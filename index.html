Anime

Anime is a japanese term for animation. It refers to the cartoon and other animations crafted in Japan. The earliest commercial anime dates back to 1917; from then on there have been progressive developing methods that have furthered the growth of anime. The fandom is grown over the years and the recent animes have set evryone on the edge of there seat. Animes such as Attack on Titan, One piece, and Promise Neverland are such examples.
